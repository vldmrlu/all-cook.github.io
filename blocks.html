How does LOW CARB diet work for weight loss?

  • 25-30% of daily energy should come from carbohydrates, 2 times less than the recommended standard diet.
  • Our bodies metabolise carbohydrates for energy and save the excess of it in the form of fat.
  • Reducing the consumption of carbs makes your body consume energy from the stored fat.
  • The rule is to consume nutrient-rich carbohydrates, such as vegetables and pulses, which our body takes longer to metabolise.
